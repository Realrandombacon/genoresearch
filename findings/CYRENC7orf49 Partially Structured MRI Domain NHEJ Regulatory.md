HPA data reveals low tissue specificity with ubiquitous expression (detected in all tissues), low cancer specificity, low immune cell specificity, and cell type-enhanced single cell specificity, suggesting constitutive expression across cell types consistent with essential DNA damage response functions required in all dividing cells.
