# C16orf6/PHAF1 - Phagosome Assembly Factor with Autophagy Function

**Date:** 2026-03-16T14:53:19.585739

**Quality Score:** 5/10 (GOOD)

## Description
422aa phagosome assembly factor 1 that plays regulatory role in autophagic activity. Forms complex with BCAS3 and associates with autophagosome formation sites during non-selective and selective autophagy. Contains PHAF1/UPF0183 protein family domain (IPR005373, PF03676). Liver-enriched expression (54.0 nTPM). High-confidence AlphaFold structure (pLDDT 86.4). Strong interaction with BCAS3 (0.947 score) and other autophagy-related proteins including WIPI2. Originally annotated as uncharacterized C16orf6, now functionally characterized as phagosome assembly factor but remains understudied.
