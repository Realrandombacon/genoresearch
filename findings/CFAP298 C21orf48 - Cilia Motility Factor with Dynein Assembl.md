## Description
CFAP298 (formerly C21orf48) is a 290 aa protein (UniProt P57076) localized to nucleoplasm, microtubules, primary cilium transition zone, centrosome, and basal body. InterPro domains include IPR021298 (Cilium/flagella-associated protein 298 family), PF11069 (positions 189-285), and PTHR13238. AlphaFold structure shows high confidence (pLDDT 86.5), indicating a well-folded stable structure. STRING analysis reveals 10 high-confidence protein interactions with dynein axonemal assembly factors: DNAAF1 (0.870), DNAAF5 (0.823), DNAAF3 (0.812), DNAAF4 (0.809), LRRC6 (0.803), SPAG1 (0.774), CCDC65 (0.747), CFAP300 (0.735), CCDC103 (0.729), and ZMYND10 (0.628). HPA shows low tissue specificity with expression detected in all tissues. ClinVar contains 72 pathogenic/likely pathogenic variants including nonsense (p.Arg147Ter), frameshift (p.Leu146fs), and missense (p.Phe168Leu) mutations, plus multiple copy number variants. Disease associations include ciliopathy, Kartagener syndrome, and primary ciliary dyskinesia (PCD). Functional evidence indicates role in motile cilium function, specifically outer dynein arm assembly and initiation of cilium motility rather than maintenance.
