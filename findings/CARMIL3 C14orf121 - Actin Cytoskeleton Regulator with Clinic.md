# CARMIL3 (C14orf121) - Actin Cytoskeleton Regulator with Clinical Variants

**Date:** 2026-03-16T00:09:33.808456

**Quality Score:** 6/10 (GOOD)

## Description
Former dark gene C14orf121 now characterized as CARMIL3 - Capping protein, Arp2/3 and myosin-I linker protein 3. Contains PH domain, leucine-rich repeats, and CARMIL C-terminal domain. Functions in actin cytoskeleton regulation and protein phosphatase 1 signaling. Interacts with TMOD4 (tropomodulin), SRGAP2, and other cytoskeletal proteins. 22 pathogenic/likely pathogenic variants in ClinVar indicate clinical significance.
