# NICOL1/C4orf48 - Brain-Enriched Neuropeptide-Like RNA-Binding Protein

**Date:** 2026-03-16T02:45:52.610194

**Quality Score:** 4/10 (MODERATE)

## Description
NICOL1 (C4orf48) encodes a 95 aa small protein (UniProt Q5BLP8) containing a neuropeptide-like domain (PF15161, residues 35-93). Functions as an mRNA 3'-UTR binding protein that targets SERPINE1, ACTA2, CCN2, and COL4A1 transcripts, potentially promoting extracellular matrix production and mRNA stabilization. Brain-enriched expression (149.7 nTPM) with additional pituitary gland expression (72.7 nTPM). Classified as predicted secreted protein but localized to cytosol/perinuclear region. Involved in spermatogenesis and lumicrine signaling pathways. No protein-protein interactions detected in STRING-DB, consistent with its role as an RNA-binding adaptor rather than scaffold protein. The neuropeptide-like domain architecture suggests potential dual function in RNA metabolism and intercellular signaling.
