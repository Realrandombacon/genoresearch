## Description

The armadillo domain mediates protein-protein interactions with trafficking machinery (RAB19), membrane remodelers (XKR9), and adhesion components (COL6A5). Testis enrichment suggests roles in spermatogenesis, potentially in acrosome formation or sperm-egg recognition. Immune cell expression implies functions in immunological synapse formation or phagocytic cup assembly. Paralog interactions with ARMH2/ARMH4 suggest functional redundancy or heteromeric complex formation.
