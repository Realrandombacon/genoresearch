## Description
SPATC1L (Spermatogenesis and centriole associated 1 like), formerly C21orf56, encodes a 340 amino acid speriolin-like protein (UniProt Q9H0A9) with conserved N-terminal (PF15058, aa 3-153) and C-terminal (PF15059, aa 195-340) domains characteristic of the speriolin family (IPR026715). HPA data reveals testis-enriched expression at 47.4 nTPM with plasma membrane and cytosolic localization, consistent with spermatogenesis function. STRING analysis identifies 10 protein interactions including SPATA6 (0.594), SUN5 (0.531), PMFBP1 (0.580), and TSGA10 (0.489) - all established spermatogenesis/centriole proteins. The interaction network suggests SPATC1L functions in the manchette apparatus or sperm head-tail coupling apparatus during spermiogenesis. Co-expression with centriole-associated proteins (CEP112, SUN5) indicates a role in centrosome/centriole dynamics during sperm differentiation. Medium-confidence interactions (0.464-0.626 scores) suggest transient regulatory associations rather than stable complex membership.
