## Description
Uncharacterized protein (292 aa) on chromosome 11q23.1. Contains conserved NKAP domain (IPR043407) and NF-kappa-B-activating protein family domain (PF15692, residues 88-163). Human-specific sequence (no orthologs in BLAST). Extensive protein interaction network with splicing/RNA processing factors (PNN pinin, DHX8 RNA helicase, SYF2 splicing factor, RP9 spliceosome component) and casein kinases (CSNK2A1, CSNK2A2). Additional interactions with mitochondrial import protein TIMM8B, tetratricopeptide protein PIH1D2, and C9orf78. Ubiquitous expression across all tissues (low tissue specificity), cytosolic localization. Classified as predicted intracellular protein. The domain architecture and interaction profile strongly suggest NKAPD1 functions as an adaptor or scaffold protein linking NF-κB signaling to RNA splicing machinery, potentially coordinating transcriptional activation with post-transcriptional processing. Casein kinase interactions indicate regulatory phosphorylation events.
