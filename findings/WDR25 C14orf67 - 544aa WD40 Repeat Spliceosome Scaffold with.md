The 14q32.32 chromosomal position overlaps with imprinted regions, and CNVs affecting WDR25 may disrupt spliceosome stoichiometry, potentially contributing to developmental or hematologic phenotypes. Despite its 'C14orf' designation, WDR25 is functionally characterized as a core spliceosomal scaffold protein.
