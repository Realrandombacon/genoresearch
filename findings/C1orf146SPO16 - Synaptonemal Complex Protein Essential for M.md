## Description
C1orf146 (alias SPO16, SCRE) encodes a 180 aa protein belonging to the SPO16 homolog family (InterPro IPR027857, Pfam PF15162, residues 10-170). Functions in synaptonemal complex assembly and reinforcement, ensuring progression of meiotic prophase I in male and female germ cells. HPA shows group-enriched expression in testis (7.0 nTPM) and retina (1.9 nTPM) with centriolar satellite localization. ClinVar contains 8 pathogenic variants including copy number losses and deletions on chromosome 1p22.1, confirming disease relevance despite no specific disease assignment. Likely functions as structural component of synaptonemal complex central element, stabilizing homologous chromosome pairing during meiosis. Represents a functionally characterized but clinically underexplored dark gene.
