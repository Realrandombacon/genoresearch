## Description

HPA data shows ubiquitous expression across all tissues with nucleoplasmic localization and low tissue specificity (nTPM data available in HPA). ClinVar contains 12 pathogenic/likely pathogenic variants, predominantly copy number losses and gains at 19p13.2, indicating dosage sensitivity. Conservation data shows orthologs in primates (chimpanzee 380aa, orangutan 394aa).
