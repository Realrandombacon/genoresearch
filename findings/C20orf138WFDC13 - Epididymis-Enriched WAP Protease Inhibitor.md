# C20orf138/WFDC13 - Epididymis-Enriched WAP Protease Inhibitor

**Date:** 2026-03-16T16:20:08.927583

## Description
Former dark gene C20orf138 encodes WFDC13, a 93aa WAP four-disulfide core domain protein. Functions as putative acid-stable serine protease inhibitor. Epididymis-enriched expression (84.5 nTPM). Interacts strongly with WFDC family proteins (WFDC9, WFDC11, WFDC10A/B) and SPINT4, suggesting coordinated role in epididymal protease regulation and sperm maturation. AlphaFold structure shows medium confidence (pLDDT 69.3) indicating partially ordered structure typical of small secreted inhibitors.
