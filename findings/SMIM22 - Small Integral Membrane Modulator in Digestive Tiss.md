## Description

Highly expressed in digestive tissues: intestine (178.3 nTPM), stomach (156.9 nTPM), salivary gland (126.4 nTPM). Localizes to nucleoplasm and cytosol. May modulate lipid droplet formation through SQLE interaction. Part of Small Integral Membrane Modulators family (IPR053081).
