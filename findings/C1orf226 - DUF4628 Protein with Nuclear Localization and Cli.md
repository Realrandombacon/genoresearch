# C1orf226 - DUF4628 Protein with Nuclear Localization and Clinical CNVs

## Description
C1orf226 encodes a 272 aa protein containing the conserved domain DUF4628 (PF15429) across its entire length. Highly conserved in mammals (80.5% identity to bovine homolog). Localizes to the nucleoplasm with highest expression in liver (20.8 nTPM). Interacts with NKAP (T-cell development), CLBA1, and TEN1 (DNA replication/repair complex). AlphaFold predicts a partially structured protein (pLDDT 56.2), suggesting potential intrinsic disorder. ClinVar reports 16 pathogenic copy number variants (gains/losses) spanning the locus, indicating strong disease relevance despite lack of specific point mutation associations. Function remains unknown but nuclear localization and TEN1 interaction suggest a role in genome maintenance or transcriptional regulation.
