## Description
C7orf76 (SEM1/DSS1) encodes a 70 amino acid essential proteasome subunit (UniProt P60896) with a shorter 128 aa isoform (Q6ZVN7). InterPro identifies the DSS1/SEM1 family (IPR007834/PF05160, aa 3-66) with GO annotations to mRNA export from nucleus (GO:0006406), proteasome assembly (GO:0043248), and proteasome regulatory particle lid subcomplex (GO:0008541). AlphaFold structure (pLDDT 69.5) shows medium confidence consistent with a small, partially disordered protein. HPA confirms ubiquitous expression across all tissues with cytosolic localization and essential protein classification. STRING interactions reveal dual functional networks: (1) 26S proteasome lid complex with PSMD11 (0.998), PSMD14 (0.997), PSMD8 (0.994), PSMD12 (0.994), PCID2 (0.994), PSMD13 (0.993), and ENY2 (0.993); (2) DNA repair linkage via BRCA2 (0.633). ClinVar contains 19 pathogenic variants predominantly as chromosomal 7q21.3 copy number losses and deletions, associated with split-hand/foot malformation and craniofacial defects. SEM1 functions as a molecular bridge: as a proteasome lid subunit, it stabilizes the RPN11/PSMD14 deubiquitinase for substrate processing; independently, it binds BRCA2\'s DNA-binding domain facilitating homologous recombination repair.
