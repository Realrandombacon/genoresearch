## Description
C10orf130 (Gene ID: 414264, chromosome 10q23.31) encodes Coiled-Coil and C2 Domain-Containing Protein 2B (CC2D2B), a large 1437 amino acid intracellular protein (UniProt Q6DHV5) with complex domain architecture. The protein contains an N-terminal DUF5523 domain (InterPro IPR041510, Pfam PF17661, residues 23-267), a CC2D2A-type N-terminal C2 domain (IPR028928, PF15625, residues 470-637), a C-terminal C2 domain (IPR000008, SM00239, residues 861-1019), a CEP76 peptidase-like domain (IPR056290, PF24656, residues 1166-1282), and a CEP76 C-terminal domain (IPR056288, PF24652, residues 1310-1432). CC2D2B belongs to the tectonic-like complex component family (IPR052434, PTHR20837), suggesting ciliary/centrosomal function. AlphaFold structure prediction yields medium confidence (pLDDT 68.1), consistent with a multi-domain scaffold protein containing both structured C2 domains and disordered linker regions. Human Protein Atlas reveals testis-enriched expression (8.1 nTPM) with subcellular localization to nucleoplasm, vesicles, basal body, and cytosol—the basal body annotation strongly implicates CC2D2B in centrosome/cilium biology. STRING interaction network identifies 10 partners, predominantly uncharacterized proteins: C3orf22 (0.581), RNASE12 (0.570), C12orf56 (0.544), SMIM17 (0.541), CCNJ (0.522), ANKRD62 (0.509), C22orf42 (0.507), C1orf167 (0.507), SH2D7 (0.506), and SPDYE4 (0.480), indicating CC2D2B operates within a dark gene interactome of poorly characterized factors. ClinVar documents 20 pathogenic/likely pathogenic variants including copy number alterations across 10q23-26 and one intronic variant (c.3631-225A>T), though no specific disease phenotype is defined. The combination of testis expression, basal body localization, C2 membrane-binding domains, and tectonic complex homology suggests CC2D2B functions as a ciliary scaffold protein potentially involved in spermatogenesis or cilia-dependent signaling, with its interaction network representing a cluster of functionally unannotated dark genes awaiting mechanistic characterization.
