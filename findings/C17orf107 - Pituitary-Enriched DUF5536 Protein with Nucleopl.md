# C17orf107 - Pituitary-Enriched DUF5536 Protein with Nucleoplasmic Localization

**Date:** 2026-03-16T01:45:14.142602

**Quality Score:** 7/10 (GOOD)

## Description
C17orf107 encodes a 190 aa uncharacterized protein containing a DUF5536 domain (PF17688, residues 1-189) covering the entire protein sequence. Expression is tissue-enhanced with highest levels in pituitary gland (24.2 nTPM) and heart muscle (14.3 nTPM), also detected in many tissues. Localized to nucleoplasm. Shows medium-confidence interactions with transcriptional repressor ETV3L (0.447), acetylcholine receptor subunit CHRNE (0.443), poly(A) binding protein PABPC1L2B (0.424), ubiquitin-binding protein UBXN10 (0.411), sperm flagellar protein SPEF1 (0.406), and cancer/testis antigen SSX7 (0.400). Note: ClinVar reports 235 pathogenic variants in this region but they map to neighboring CHRNE gene (cholinergic receptor epsilon), not C17orf107 itself - this is a genomic proximity artifact. Potential roles in pituitary hormone regulation, transcriptional control, or nuclear RNA metabolism.
