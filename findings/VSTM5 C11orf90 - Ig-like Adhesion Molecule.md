## Description
VSTM5 is a single-pass transmembrane protein containing a V-set Ig-like domain. BLAST analysis reveals significant homology to Junctional Adhesion Molecule A (JAM-A) and GlialCAM, placing it in the Ig-superfamily of cell adhesion molecules. Likely functions in cell-cell junction formation, filopodia dynamics, and excitatory synapse assembly in the nervous system, analogous to its homologs.
