# FAM83A: PLD-like Scaffolding Protein Anchoring Casein Kinase 1 to Regulate EGFR Signaling and Cell Proliferation

**Date:** 2026-03-18T01:26:44.541587

**Quality Score:** 10/10 (EXCELLENT)

## Description
FAM83A (alias SACK1A, BJ-TSA-9) encodes a 434 amino acid scaffolding CK1 anchoring protein A (UniProt Q86UY5) containing an N-terminal phospholipase D-like domain (cd09181, residues 24-298), CK1 scaffolding anchor domain (IPR012461, PF07894, residues 20-295), and belongs to the FAM83 family (IPR050944, PTHR16181, residues 9-360). The protein adopts an endonuclease-like fold (G3DSA:3.30.870.10, residues 120-304) with phospholipase D/nuclease superfamily architecture (SSF56024, residues 144-296). AlphaFold predicts a well-folded structure with confident score (pLDDT 72.4). HPA data could not be resolved, but gene summary indicates cytoplasmic localization. STRING interactions reveal associations with casein kinase 1 isoforms CSNK1A1 (0.599), CSNK1E (0.452), Rab GTPase regulator TBC1D31 (0.512), mitochondrial ribosomal protein MRPL23 (0.501), Rab5 interacting factor RAB5IF (0.470), p53 target TP53I13 (0.463), ERAD component DERL1 (0.438), transcriptional repressor ZHX3 (0.425), and RNA helicase DHX35 (0.421). ClinVar reports 29 pathogenic variants including copy number gains/losses across 8q regions and 3 UTR variant (c.*875T>A). The gene is conserved across vertebrates: mouse (Q8K2P2, 436 aa, ~95% identity), xenopus (Q66JF7, 441 aa, ~85% identity).
