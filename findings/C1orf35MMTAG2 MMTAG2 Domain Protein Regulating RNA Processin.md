# C1orf35/MMTAG2: MMTAG2 Domain Protein Regulating RNA Processing and Spliceosome Assembly

**Date:** 2026-03-18T01:21:47.528190

**Quality Score:** 10/10 (EXCELLENT)

## Description
C1orf35 (alias MMTAG2) encodes a 263 amino acid multiple myeloma tumor-associated protein 2 (UniProt Q9BU76) containing an MMTAG2 N-terminal domain (IPR019315, PF10159, residues 8-83) and MMTAG2-like family domain (IPR039207, residues 1-251). AlphaFold predicts a partially structured protein with medium confidence (pLDDT 62.8), suggesting intrinsically disordered regions. HPA data shows ubiquitous expression with nuclear speckles, plasma membrane, and cytosol localization. STRING interactions reveal associations with RNA-binding and splicing factors: TARBP2 (0.514), RBMX2 (0.506), PRKRA (0.501), SRPK2 (0.452), and chromatin regulators BRD2 (0.458), SNIP1 (0.433). ClinVar reports 38 pathogenic variants including copy number gains/losses, deletions, and splice site variants (c.533+84C>T). The gene is conserved across vertebrates: cattle (A0A3Q1LPS7, 323 aa, ~70% identity), lancelet (A0A8J9ZQH1, 335 aa, ~65% identity).
