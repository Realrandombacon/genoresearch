## Description
331aa protein regulating TPGC complex for cytoskeletal organization, nuclear shape, and cilium disassembly. Localized to centriolar satellites, primary cilium, basal body, and cytosol. Contains dimerization/docking domain (aa 13-68) and CSTPP1 N-terminal domain (aa 9-57). Highly conserved across vertebrates (93-99% identity in mammals, 56% in zebrafish). AlphaFold structure shows high confidence (pLDDT 80.1). ClinVar: 15 pathogenic/likely pathogenic variants identified though specific diseases not yet assigned. Expressed ubiquitously with cell type enhancement. Not a true dark gene - function characterized but clinical phenotypes remain unclear.
