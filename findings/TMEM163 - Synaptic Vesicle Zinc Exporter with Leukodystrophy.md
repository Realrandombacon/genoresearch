## Description
TMEM163 encodes a 289aa multi-pass transmembrane protein with cation efflux domains (G3DSA:1.20.1510.10, IPR026765, IPR027469, positions 86-278) characteristic of zinc transporters. The AlphaFold structure (pLDDT 76.5) shows high confidence with well-ordered transmembrane helices. HPA data reveals brain-enriched expression (44.4 nTPM) with secondary expression in pancreas (26.3 nTPM), consistent with its alias SV31 (synaptic vesicle protein 31). STRING interactions connect TMEM163 to lysosomal/vesicular trafficking partners: TMEM175 (0.505, lysosomal K+ channel), ACMSD (0.571, tryptophan metabolism), and MOXD1 (0.434, monoxygenase). ClinVar contains 19 pathogenic/likely pathogenic variants including missense mutations (p.Arg138His, p.Glu80Asp, p.His146Arg) and copy number gains, with disease implications in hypomyelinating leukodystrophy 25 (HLD25). Conservation data shows orthologs in mouse (288aa, 94% identity) and rat (288aa, 93% identity). The mechanistic hypothesis: TMEM163 functions as a synaptic vesicle zinc exporter that regulates intravesicular zinc concentration, affecting neurotransmitter packaging and release; dysfunction leads to myelination defects via disrupted zinc homeostasis in oligodendrocytes.
