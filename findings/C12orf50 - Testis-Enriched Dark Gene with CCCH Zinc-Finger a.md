True dark gene: CCCH zinc-finger/DUF5571 dual domain architecture suggesting RNA binding plus unknown function, testis-enriched expression (spermatogenesis signature), dark gene interaction network (C12orf29/C20orf173/CXorf65), AKAP3 partnership indicating cAMP signaling potential, clinically implicated with 12q CNV burden and truncating variants, but molecular mechanism completely unknown. Hypothesis: may function as a testis-specific RNA-binding protein that coordinates post-transcriptional regulation during spermatogenesis (testis enrichment + CCCH zinc-finger), using DUF5571 domain for protein scaffolding or RNA recognition, with AKAP3 interaction suggesting cAMP-dependent phosphorylation regulation and dark gene network partnerships indicating coordinated uncharacterized protein complex assembly, potentially serving as spermatogenic checkpoint factor with dysregulation contributing to 12q deletion syndromes with reproductive/developmental phenotypes.
