Contains multiple ankyrin repeat clusters (aa 258-795) for protein-protein interactions, C-terminal MYND-type zinc finger (aa 880-920) for chromatin/DNA binding, and N-terminal MORN repeats (aa 1-91) suggesting membrane association. Strong evolutionary conservation (67.5% identity with mouse). High-confidence AlphaFold structure (pLDDT 77.8). Interacts with germ cell factors including ZAR1 (oocyte/early embryo), THAP4 (zinc finger transcription factor), and MKRN2OS. Nuclear localization (nucleoplasm, nuclear bodies). Likely functions as testis-specific chromatin modifier or transcriptional coregulator during spermatogenesis.
