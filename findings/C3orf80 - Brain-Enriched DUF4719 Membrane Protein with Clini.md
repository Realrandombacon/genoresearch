The membrane localization prediction combined with brain enrichment positions C3orf80 as a potential neuronal membrane scaffold. Clinically, C3orf80 harbors 21 pathogenic/likely pathogenic variants in ClinVar, predominantly copy number variations (CNVs) spanning chromosome 3q regions. These large-scale duplications and deletions implicate C3orf80 dosage sensitivity in disease pathogenesis, though specific phenotypes remain undefined due to the broad CNV breakpoints. Conservation across mammals (mouse, marmoset, macaque, pig) confirms functional constraint.
