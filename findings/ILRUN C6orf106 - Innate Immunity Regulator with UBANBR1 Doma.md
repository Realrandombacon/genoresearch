# ILRUN (C6orf106) - Innate Immunity Regulator with UBA/NBR1 Domains

## Description
Dark gene ILRUN encodes a 298 aa protein that negatively regulates innate antiviral response by blocking IRF3-dependent cytokine production (IFNA, IFNB, TNF). Contains conserved UBA-like domain (aa 23-64) for ubiquitin binding and NBR1-like FW domain (aa 71-180) suggesting role in autophagy regulation. Highly expressed in skeletal muscle (202.1 nTPM), localizes to nuclear speckles/centrosome/cytosol. Interacts with SPDEF, SMIM29, NUDT3, HSPBP1. AlphaFold structure confident (pLDDT 71.2). ClinVar shows 5 pathogenic CNVs on chromosome 6. Homology across vertebrates (human, mouse, chicken, zebrafish, rat).
