## Description
C11orf43 (Gene ID: 492304) was annotated as chromosome 11 open reading frame 43 with aliases FLJ22066 and pp9974, but comprehensive analysis reveals it encodes IGF2 (Insulin-like Growth Factor 2), a well-characterized 180 aa secreted hormone. UniProt accession P01344 contains insulin-like growth factor domains (IPR016179, positions 30-84), insulin/IGF/relaxin family motifs (PF00049, positions 57-84), and IGF-II E-peptide region (IPR013576, positions 112-166). STRING interactions show high-confidence binding to IGF1R receptor (0.999), IGF1 ligand (0.998), IGF2BP2 RNA-binding protein (0.994), IGFBP3 binding protein (0.960), and IGF2R clearance receptor (0.743), indicating central role in growth signaling pathway. HPA shows tissue-enriched expression with placenta having extremely high expression (2597.9 nTPM), classified as plasma protein, growth factor, hormone, and mitogen with cancer-related and dwarfism disease involvement. ClinVar contains 86 pathogenic variants including copy number gains on 11p15.5 and missense mutations (p.Arg156Ser, p.Phe50Cys) associated with Beckwith-Wiedemann syndrome and growth disorders. AlphaFold structure (pLDDT 59.0) indicates partially structured hormone peptide. This gene should NOT be classified as "dark" - it has established function as fetal growth factor binding IGF1R to activate PI3K-AKT and MAPK signaling pathways, with genomic imprinting at 11p15.5 locus.
