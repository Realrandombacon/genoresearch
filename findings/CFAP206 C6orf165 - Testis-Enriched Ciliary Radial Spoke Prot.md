## Description
CFAP206 encodes a 622 aa cilia- and flagella-associated protein (UniProt Q8IYR0) with high-confidence AlphaFold structure (pLDDT 85.4). InterPro domains include IPR021897 (CFAP206 family, residues 5-620) and PF12018 (DUF, residues 215-491), confirming membership in the ciliary protein superfamily. HPA reveals testis-enriched expression (55.2 nTPM) with secondary expression in fallopian tube (21.0 nTPM), consistent with role in motile cilia/flagella. STRING interactions identify 10 high-confidence partners including CCDC113 (0.707), SMIM8 (0.551), EFCAB10 (0.530), CFAP61 (0.508), RSPH4A (0.495), and DRC1 (0.445) - all established axonemal/radial spoke components. ClinVar documents 20 pathogenic/likely pathogenic variants including frameshift mutations (p.Asn477fs) and copy number losses at 6q15, implicating CFAP206 in human disease. Conservation across mouse (Q6PE87), zebrafish (A2RV06), bovine (Q29RL1), and Tetrahymena (Q23H79) indicates ancient ciliary function.
