# C1orf115/RDD1 - Intestine-Enriched Drug-Induced Death Protein with Ciliary Localization

**Date:** 2026-03-16T15:34:46.458615

**Quality Score:** 9/10 (EXCELLENT)

## Description
142aa protein containing DDDP domain (Required for Drug-induced Death Protein). Intestine-enriched expression (113.9 nTPM) with immune cell enhancement. Localized to 9+0 non-motile cilium. Functionally linked to ABCB1 drug efflux modulation. Evolutionarily conserved (71% identity in rat, 70% in mouse). STRING interactions with MARC2, TMEM244, RASGEF1C, LYZL4 suggest roles in cellular stress response. clinically significant with 30 pathogenic/likely pathogenic variants in ClinVar including copy number variations.
