## Description
TOMM5 (C9orf105) encodes a 51-amino acid mitochondrial outer membrane translocase subunit. Orthologs exist across vertebrates (salmon, pike, bullfrog, sablefish) all maintaining exactly 51aa length, indicating strong purifying selection. This tiny protein serves as a critical adaptor in the TOM complex, bridging TOMM22 and TOMM40 to facilitate precursor protein recognition and membrane insertion.
