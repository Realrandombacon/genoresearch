## Description
C11orf68 (UniProt Q9H3H3) encodes a 292aa nucleolar protein on chromosome 11q13.1 with predicted involvement in cap-dependent translation regulation and RNA metabolism. InterPro analysis reveals a canonical eIF4E-like RNA cap-binding fold (G3DSA:3.30.760.10/SSF55418/IPR023398, aa 59-292) embedded within the Basophilic leukemia-expressed protein Bles03 family scaffold (IPR015034/PF08939/PTHR31977, aa 10-291), with a defined Bles03 domain (PF08939, aa 155-253) characteristic of leukemia-associated RNA-binding proteins. AlphaFold structure prediction shows high confidence (pLDDT 82.2), indicating a well-folded eIF4E-like β-barrel structure capable of m7GTP cap recognition. HPA data demonstrates ubiquitous expression (detected in all tissues) with low tissue specificity, localized to nucleoplasm, nucleoli, and nucleoli rim—subcompartments critical for ribosome biogenesis and rRNA processing—suggesting roles in translational control and ribosome assembly. STRING interaction network reveals 6 medium-confidence partners enriched for RNA binding and translation regulation: EVA1B (0.470, epithelial cell transformation sequence), CSDE1 (0.461, cold-shock domain RNA-binding protein), NONO (0.459, non-POU domain RNA-binding transcription factor), DRAP1 (0.431, DR1-associated protein transcriptional repressor), EIF4H (0.403, eukaryotic translation initiation factor), FICD (0.401, tRNA modification enzyme). ClinVar contains 11 pathogenic/likely pathogenic copy number variants including 11q12.1-13.5 duplications (amplicons), 11q13.1-13.2 gains, and 11p15.5-q25 amplifications, indicating extreme dosage sensitivity and oncogenic potential in 11q amplification syndromes and leukemia. Conservation spans the Bles03 protein family across vertebrates. Sequence composition shows moderate charge and hydrophobicity consistent with structured RNA-binding domains. Mechanistic hypothesis: C11orf68 functions as a nucleolar eIF4E-like cap-binding protein that recognizes m7GTP caps on nascent pre-rRNA transcripts, recruiting RNA helicases (EVA1B, CSDE1) and transcriptional co-repressors (NONO, DRAP1) to regulate ribosome biogenesis and cap-dependent translation; copy number gains in leukemia drive aberrant ribosome production and oncogenic translation programs via Bles03-mediated RNA scaffold activity.
