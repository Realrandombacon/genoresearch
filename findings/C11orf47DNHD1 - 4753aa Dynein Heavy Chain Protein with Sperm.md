## Description
C11orf47 (now characterized as DNHD1) encodes a massive 4753 amino acid dynein heavy chain domain-containing protein (UniProt Q96M86). InterPro analysis reveals extensive dynein motor domains including IPR004273 (Dynein heavy chain D6 P-loop domain, residues 4101-4228), IPR013602 (Dynein heavy chain linker, 1017-1464), IPR024317 (AAA module D4, 2865-3124), IPR024743 (coiled coil stalk, 3225-3465), and IPR026983 (Dynein heavy chain family). These domains confer minus-end-directed microtubule motor activity (GO:0008569). AlphaFold predicts high-confidence structure (pLDDT 88.4). STRING interactions reveal axonemal dynein complex partners: DNAI1 (0.758), WDR63 (0.703), WDR78 (0.702), DNAI2 (0.700), CCDC114 (0.681), DNAL1 (0.634), DNAH1 (0.623), CASC1 (0.592), DNAH2 (0.586), NME8 (0.574) - all consistent with ciliary/flagellar dynein assembly. HPA shows low tissue specificity with subcellular localization to sperm midpiece and annulus, indicating role in sperm flagellum motility. ClinVar documents 109 pathogenic/likely pathogenic variants including frameshifts (c.3801dup p.Leu1268fs, c.3278del p.Leu1093fs, c.9655del p.Ala3219fs) and splice variants, suggesting clinical relevance for ciliopathy or male infertility phenotypes. Conservation is expected to be high across vertebrates given dynein's essential motor function.
