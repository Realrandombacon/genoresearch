# FMNL1 - Formin-like Protein 1: Cytoskeletal Regulator in Immune Cells

**Date:** 2026-03-16T12:51:21.117916

**Quality Score:** 7/10 (GOOD)

## Description
FMNL1 encodes a 1100aa formin family protein with FH2, FH3, and GTPase-binding domains. Functions in actin cytoskeleton organization, cell motility, and macrophage survival. Highly expressed in bone marrow (255.3 nTPM) and lymphoid tissue. Interacts with Rho GTPases (CDC42, RAC1, RHOA) and profilins. Contains 12 pathogenic ClinVar variants (mostly CNVs). NOT a dark gene - well-characterized cytoskeletal regulator.
