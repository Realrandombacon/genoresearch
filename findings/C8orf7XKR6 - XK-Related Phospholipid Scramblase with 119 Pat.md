## Description
C8orf7 (now designated XKR6) encodes a 641 amino acid XK-related protein 6 localized to cellular membranes. InterPro analysis reveals two major domain signatures: IPR018629/XK-related protein domain (PF09815, residues 130-500) and IPR050895/XK-related phospholipid scramblase domain (residues 115-601), indicating this protein functions as a membrane-associated phospholipid scramblase that translocates phospholipids between membrane leaflets. AlphaFold structure prediction (AF-Q5GH73-F1) shows medium confidence (pLDDT 62.8) across the full 641 aa sequence, consistent with a transmembrane protein containing both ordered domains and flexible regions. STRING-DB identifies 10 protein interaction partners including FAM167A (0.661), SLC35G5 (0.580), LCA5L (0.529), CCM2 (0.528), KEL (0.505), PPP1R3B (0.499), MSRA (0.495), BLK (0.467), TNKS (0.451), and MTMR9 (0.408), suggesting roles in membrane trafficking and signaling complexes. ClinVar reports 119 pathogenic/likely pathogenic variants, predominantly copy number losses and gains spanning 8p23 regions, indicating strong disease association. The gene lacks HPA expression data but InterPro GO annotations confirm membrane localization (GO:0016020). Panther classification PTHR16024 places this in the XK-related phospholipid scramblase family, homologous to XKR8 which mediates phosphatidylserine exposure during apoptosis.
